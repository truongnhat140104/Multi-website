Ticket Information & FAQ

A general admission ticket (Weekend or Day ticket) gives you access to all live demonstration stages, entry to the main food markets, farm walks, garden tours, and the evening musical acts/bar areas. It does not include access to hands-on workshops, wine/cocktail masterclasses, or food purchases at food trucks, which must be purchased separately.

The festival grounds open at 10:00 AM daily and close at 11:30 PM on Friday and Saturday, and 8:00 PM on Sunday. Last entry to the parking lot and festival site is 2 hours before closing. Please bring your digital or printed tickets for scanning at the gate. As this is an outdoor event held in East Cork gardens, we advise dressing for all Irish weather conditions!

Children under the age of 12 enter free when accompanied by a paying adult, but they must still hold a valid "Under 12 Child Ticket" which can be added to your cart for free during booking. Our Family Day Ticket covers entry for 2 Adults and up to 3 Children (under 12) for either Saturday or Sunday, offering great savings.

Tickets are non-refundable once purchased. However, you can transfer your ticket to another person or exchange it for a different day (subject to availability) up to 14 days before the event. If the event is canceled due to unforeseen emergency circumstances, full refunds will be issued to all ticket buyers.

Ballymaloe House grounds are partially accessible. Most demonstration stages and food markets are located on flat, packed gravel paths or grassy fields. Accessible parking is available close to the main gate for blue-badge holders. Accessible restrooms are located in the Grainstore building and at designated areas around the field.

If you purchase a Live Stream Pass, you will receive a unique access link and code via email 48 hours before the event starts. The stream will cover the main demo stage in real time, and you will also receive access to on-demand recordings of the sessions for 30 days after the festival ends.
