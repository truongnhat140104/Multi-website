About Chinese Culture Festival

Message

Hong Kong is deeply rooted in the profound Chinese culture, at the same time enjoys the unique advantage of being a melting pot of Chinese and Western cultures with strong global connections.

With the support of our country, we are committed to giving full play to Hong Kong’s role as an East-meets-West centre for international cultural exchange, showcasing our country’s fine traditions to the world in a rich diversity of ways.

The Chinese Culture Festival preserves and promotes Chinese culture with innovative ideas and in contemporary language, enabling citizens and visitors of all ages and backgrounds to experience the timeless charm and contemporary vitality of Chinese cultural traditions.

This year’s Festival features a fine selection of programmes and activities based on Chinese legends, history, opera, dance, music, exhibitions and multi-arts performances.

We invite audiences to join the Festival and experience the enduring and ever-renewing charm of Chinese culture.

Chinese Culture Festival

The Leisure and Cultural Services Department organizes the Chinese Culture Festival to showcase the richness and diversity of Chinese culture through a variety of performing arts programmes, exhibitions and extension activities.

Chinese Culture Promotion Office

The Chinese Culture Promotion Office is established to plan and coordinate Chinese cultural programmes and activities, with a view to strengthening community awareness and appreciation of traditional Chinese culture.

Theme Music of Chinese Culture Festival

The theme music of the Chinese Culture Festival reflects the Festival’s mission of presenting the richness, diversity and contemporary vitality of Chinese culture.

The composition combines traditional Chinese musical elements with a modern arrangement, creating a piece that is accessible to audiences of different ages and cultural backgrounds.

It is a great privilege to be commissioned to write the theme music for the Chinese Culture Festival. I wish with all my heart that the Festival will be a great success.

Chris Babida

Composer, Arranger and Producer of
Chinese Culture Festival Theme Music
